Bio

Certificates- NASM CPT

Being active has been a part of my life since I was a kid.

As a kid I always part of some sort of team sport during the school year. Summers were for the pool and pick-up sports with friends. From these early sports I learned that moving could be FUN! In high school I started to dabble in weight lifting because at 6’ and 140lbs I could use a little meat on my bones and also. . . girls. I continued the pick-up sports and unstructured weight lifting into college and then found the sport of Ultimate Frisbee (Yes! It is a sport, I promise!). Through Ultimate Frisbee I meet two friends who planted the seed that grew into a fascination of how the body adapts and improves through being stressed, aka workingout. For years after I did my own research into the fitness world. During this time, I became a rock-climbing coach. Through my coaching I found a love to see people grow and improve. It was only a matter of time before I decided I would get my personal training certificate.

I can’t wait to discover your fitness goals and to be able to help guild you through your fitness journey.

Services

We all have different levels of commitment (both time and financial), and that is ok. It is my goal to find a fitness program that works for YOU. For this reason, I have a wide range of services to meet your needs.

Pre-Built Workout Plans

If you are just looking for a basic plan this is for you. Get a pre-built 6-week plan with a focus on either- Cardio endurance, Cardio power, Strength endurance, Maximum Strength, Strength power, getting jacked, fully body stability, and 6-pack abs. Each focus has three different fitness level-based plan; beginner, Intermediate and Advance.

    What to expect for each level
  • Beginner:Three days a week. Each workout around 30min (this will vary based on you), not including warm-up or cool down.
  • Intermediate:Three days a week. Each workout is around 45min (this will vary based on you), not including warm-up or cool down.
  • Advance:Four days a week. Each workout is around 1 hour (this will vary based on you), not including warm-up or cool down.

Personalized workout plan

If you have specific fitness goals, and the motivation to stick to a plan, this is the plan for you. I will meet with you via zoom for 1 hour to discuss your goals and conduct a basic fitness assessment. You will receive a 6-week plan along with warm-up and cool-down suggestions all based on your assessment.

Personalized workout plan W/ check-ins

If you have specific fitness goals, but need the motivation and accountability of a coach, this is for you. All the perks of the personalize workout plan PLUS a 30 min check-in each week.

One-on-One Web session

If you need a coach’s motivation to help you give your all, look no further than this option. Get a 45-min coaching session with a coach via zoom. You don’t need to leave you house! Price will depend on time needed to build out each workout.

One-on-One in person session

For those that need a coach’s motivation to help you give your all and need the coach by your side. Get a 45-min in person coaching session. No gear needed; I will provide all that is needed. Price will depend on time needed to build out each workout.
